# Client Discovery Brief Generator

A free Claude Skill by AI Courses by Green School that turns a vague client enquiry — or your messy discovery-call notes — into a clear brief, the right questions to ask, and a pricing approach. Built so Australian freelancers scope properly and stop undercharging.

## Who it's for

Australian freelancers and small studios — design, development, marketing, copy, video, consulting — working with AU small business, corporate, and international clients.

## What you get

1. 15-20 sharp questions to ask before or during the call.
2. The client's request broken into concrete deliverables, with in-scope and out-of-scope spelled out.
3. The risks and red flags hiding in how the client is talking.
4. The parts most likely to balloon mid-project — and wording to protect you.
5. Three pricing structures with realistic AU context, and how to handle GST.
6. A contract structure outline (payment terms, revisions, change orders, cancellation).
7. A ready-to-send follow-up email confirming scope and next steps.

It's blunt about bad-client signals — that's the value.

## How to use it (non-technical)

1. Open Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app) with this skill added (see "Installing the skill" below).
2. Paste the client's message or your call notes. Add your service type and usual rate for a sharper result.
3. Use the questions on the call, send the follow-up email, and quote off the scoped brief — not a guess.

You don't need to name the skill — Claude recognises a client / scoping / pricing request.

## Installing the skill

Full step-by-step instructions for every platform (Claude.ai, Claude Desktop, Claude Code) are in the repository's [INSTALL.md](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/blob/main/INSTALL.md).

**The short version (no technical steps):**

1. **[⬇️ Download the skill (ZIP)](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/raw/main/skills/client-discovery-brief/client-discovery-brief.zip)** — one ready-to-upload file. You don't need to build or zip anything yourself.
2. In Claude, turn on **Code execution and file creation** under **Settings → Capabilities** (one-time).
3. Go to **Customize → Skills**, click **"+" → Create skill → Upload a skill**, and upload the ZIP you just downloaded.
4. Toggle the skill on. Done — start a new chat and paste a client enquiry or your call notes.

## What it won't do

It outlines a contract structure but isn't legal advice — have a lawyer review binding terms. Pricing ranges are indicative, not guarantees.
