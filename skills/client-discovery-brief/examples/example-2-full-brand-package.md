# Example 2 — "The Full Brand Package" (solo founder)

**Scenario (synthetic).** A solo founder messages a freelance brand designer: "I'm launching my startup and need the full brand package — logo, colours, the works. What do you charge?"

---

## OUTPUT (what the skill returns)

### 1. Diagnostic questions (15-20) — ask before quoting
- What's the business, and who's the customer?
- Is there an existing name, or is naming part of this?
- What does "the works" include to you — logo, palette, typography, brand guidelines, business cards, social templates, website, packaging?
- Launch date, and what's driving it?
- Budget range?
- Sole decision-maker, or are there investors / co-founders with input?
- Any existing visual ideas or references?
- Where will the brand live first (web, social, print, product)?
- Will you need ongoing design support after launch?
- Do you have copy and photography, or is that needed too?

### 2. Request decomposed into deliverables
- Likely in scope: primary logo + variations, colour palette, typography, basic brand guidelines.
- Often assumed but must be confirmed: social templates, business cards, website, packaging, naming.
- Success criteria: a consistent, usable identity the founder can apply themselves.
- Dependencies: founder provides business direction, references, and timely feedback.

### 3. Risks and red flags
- "The works" is undefined and means different things to everyone — the biggest risk.
- Solo founder, pre-launch: budget may be tight and emotions high.
- "What do you charge?" before scope = being pushed to anchor a number blind.

### 4. Scope-creep risk areas
- Unlimited logo concepts and revisions.
- "Can you also do the website / pitch deck / packaging?"
- Naming creeping in unpaid. Contract phrasing: define exact deliverables and concept / revision counts.

### 5. Three pricing structures (AU context)
- Fixed package — define tiers (e.g. Essentials / Standard / Full) so the founder self-selects to budget. Indicative AU range for a solo-freelancer brand identity: ~$2,000-8,000+ AUD by scope.
- Phased — identity first, collateral later, as cash allows.
- Value-based — if the brand underpins a funded launch, price to the value, not the hours.
GST once over the $75k AUD threshold.

### 6. Contract structure outline
- Payment: 50% deposit, 50% on final files (or phased).
- A defined number of initial concepts and revision rounds.
- Change orders for added collateral.
- Clear handover of final files and IP (intellectual property) ownership on full payment.
- Kill fee if cancelled after concepts are presented.
