# Lead Magnet Builder

A free Claude Skill by AI Courses by Green School that takes you from "I should be collecting emails" to a complete, ready-to-launch lead magnet — the offer, the opt-in page copy, and the first email — in one sitting. Built for Australian small businesses, in AUD and Australian context.

## Who it's for

Australian small business owners, freelancers, coaches, and creators who want an email list but don't know what to give away, how to word the opt-in, or what to send once someone signs up.

## What you get

A complete, ship-this-week package — not a strategy lecture:

1. **The offer** — the right lead magnet format and topic for your audience and buyer stage.
2. **The opt-in page copy** — headline options, "what's inside" bullets, and a button that actually gets clicked.
3. **The first email** — the delivery email people open immediately, with subject-line options.
4. **A short nurture arc** — a simple 4-email follow-up so leads don't go cold.

## How to use it (non-technical)

1. Open Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app) with this skill added (see "Installing the skill").
2. Tell it what your business does and who you want on your list — "I'm a bookkeeper for tradies and want more leads".
3. Answer a question or two, then build out the magnet, page, and email it gives you.

You don't need to name the skill — Claude recognises a lead magnet, opt-in, freebie, or "what should I give away for emails" request.

## Installing the skill

Full per-platform steps are in the repository's [INSTALL.md](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/blob/main/INSTALL.md).

**The short version:**

1. **[⬇️ Download the skill (ZIP)](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/raw/main/skills/lead-magnet-builder/lead-magnet-builder.zip)** — one ready-to-upload file.
2. In Claude, turn on **Code execution and file creation** under **Settings → Capabilities** (one-time).
3. Go to **Customize → Skills**, click **"+" → Create skill → Upload a skill**, and upload the ZIP.
4. Toggle it on, start a new chat, and describe your business and your list goal.

## What it won't do

It won't invent download counts or testimonials for your opt-in page — those must be real (Australian Consumer Law and the Spam Act apply). And it plans and writes; it doesn't send — you'll paste the result into your email tool (Mailchimp, Kit, MailerLite, etc.).
