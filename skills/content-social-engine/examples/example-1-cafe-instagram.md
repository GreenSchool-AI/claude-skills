# Example 1 — Café that "should be posting" but never knows what

**Scenario (synthetic).** "I run a café in Newcastle. I'm on Instagram and Facebook but I post randomly — a coffee photo here, a 'we're open' there. I don't have time for a big plan. What should I actually post?"

---

## What the skill produces

**Quick context:** local B2C; goal = foot traffic + regulars; platforms = Instagram + Facebook; time = ~2 hrs/week. Lead shareable + local.

### 1. Content pillars (4)
- **The food & coffee** (35%) — the hero shots, specials, new menu items.
- **Behind-the-scenes** (25%) — the roast, the early starts, the team, sourcing.
- **Local & community** (20%) — Newcastle events, nearby businesses, "your local".
- **Personality** (15%) — opinions (oat vs full cream), little stories. (Promo folded in at ~5%.)

### 2. Starter plan (sample of 8)
- "The 6am ritual before you're even awake" (BTS, Reel, awareness) — hook: *"This happens before your first coffee."*
- "Why our flat white tastes different" (food, carousel, consideration) — hook: *"It's not the machine."*
- "Newcastle's best spot for a quiet morning?" (local, post, awareness) — hook: *"Locals, settle this for us."*
- "New autumn menu drop" (food, Reel + Story, decision).
- Customer regular spotlight (community, post, proof).
- "Oat milk: worth it or overrated?" (personality, post, engagement).
- Behind the counter on a Saturday rush (BTS, Reel).
- Loyalty card reminder (promo, Story).

### 3. Two posts written
**Reel caption:** *The 6am ritual before you're even awake* — "While Newcastle sleeps, we're firing up the grinder… [3 quick cuts: lights on, beans, first pour]. Worth getting up for? Swing by before 8 and judge for yourself. ☕"
**Engagement post:** "Hot take: oat milk belongs in a flat white and we'll die on this hill. Agree? Riot in the comments. 👇"

### 4. Weekly rhythm (~2 hrs, batched Monday)
- **Mon** IG Reel (food/BTS) + FB share. **Wed** IG carousel or local post. **Fri** Story + engagement post. Daily: reply to every comment/DM.

### 5. Idea bank (pull from later)
Latte art attempts ranked · meet the team · how we choose beans · "order like a regular" · rainy-day specials · a customer's usual · EOFY catch-up spot · school-holiday family mornings · the worst coffee order we've seen · behind a new menu item.

**Kept honest:** spotlight customers with permission; any "best in Newcastle" claim is opinion, framed as such.
