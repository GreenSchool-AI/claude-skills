# Marketing Psychology & Mental Models

A free Claude Skill by AI Courses by Green School that puts behavioural science to work on your marketing. Describe a real problem — a page that won't convert, a price people resist, an offer that falls flat — and get the two or three psychological principles that actually apply, with concrete Australian examples you can use this week.

## Who it's for

Australian small business owners, founders, marketers, and freelancers who want their marketing to work better — without a psychology degree or a manipulative bone in their body.

## What you get

Not a 60-item lecture. You bring a situation; the skill picks the few mental models that fit and turns them into action:

- The principles behind why your customers are (or aren't) acting.
- A concrete, AUD-priced application for your exact challenge.
- Pricing, trust, urgency, onboarding, and retention angles you hadn't considered.
- A built-in problem → model map, so the advice is targeted, not generic.

It's deliberately ethical: real scarcity, honest social proof, genuine guarantees — never dark patterns.

## How to use it (non-technical)

1. Open Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app) with this skill added (see "Installing the skill").
2. Describe your situation plainly: "people add to cart then leave", "my prices scare people off", "no one opens my emails".
3. Answer a quick question or two about your product and audience, then act on the specific suggestions.

You don't need to name the skill — Claude recognises a marketing-psychology, persuasion, or "why won't people buy" request.

## Installing the skill

Full per-platform steps are in the repository's [INSTALL.md](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/blob/main/INSTALL.md).

**The short version:**

1. **[⬇️ Download the skill (ZIP)](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/raw/main/skills/marketing-psychology/marketing-psychology.zip)** — one ready-to-upload file.
2. In Claude, turn on **Code execution and file creation** under **Settings → Capabilities** (one-time).
3. Go to **Customize → Skills**, click **"+" → Create skill → Upload a skill**, and upload the ZIP.
4. Toggle it on, start a new chat, and describe your marketing challenge.

## What it won't do

It won't help you build fake urgency, fake reviews, or pressure tactics — those backfire and can breach Australian Consumer Law. And psychology sharpens a real offer; it won't rescue a product people don't want.
