## OUTPUT (what the skill returns)

### 1. ATS Readiness Score: 4/10
Your resume scores 4/10 for ATS readiness. The experience is real, but it's hidden behind weak phrasing, overseas formatting, and missing keywords. Main issues below.

### 2. Top 5 issues found
- ❌ Photo, date of birth, and marital status are present. AU convention: remove all three — they invite bias and aren't expected here.
- ❌ The summary reads like a US one-pager. Expand to the AU norm of 2-3 pages and keep "Month YYYY" dates.
- ❌ Bullets describe duties ("responsible for"), not achievements. Recruiters and the ATS both reward quantified results.
- ❌ Missing the job ad's core keywords: fixed plant, RCA, CMMS / SAP PM, stakeholder management. An ATS scanning for these won't surface you.
- ❌ Ten years of overseas seniority is being undersold — the seniority is genuine; the language is junior.
