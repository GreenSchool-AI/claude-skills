# Business Idea Validator

A free Claude Skill by AI Courses by Green School that stress-tests your small business idea for the Australian market — before you spend money or hand in your notice. Honest, specific, and built around how things actually work in AU.

## Who it's for

Aspiring and early-stage Australian small business owners — beauty, health, trades, local services, online retail, tutoring — often still in a day job and weighing up whether the idea is real.

## What you get

1. A straight verdict: viable, worth testing, or probably not — with the main reason.
2. A market snapshot for Australia: rough market size, how crowded it is, and named AU competitors.
3. Who your ideal customer is, and the one reason they'd choose you — including where AI gives you an edge.
4. Three cheap, fast ways to launch a minimum version.
5. A pricing approach with a realistic AUD range.
6. The top 5 risks, each with a way to reduce it.
7. The AU regulatory basics — ABN, GST, licences, insurance — and where to check.
8. Cheap experiments to test demand before you spend, ten customer questions that actually work, and clear "stop" signals.
9. Your first week of action steps.

It won't cheerlead. If the idea is weak, it tells you why — which is the point.

## How to use it (non-technical)

1. Open Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app) with this skill added (see "Installing the skill" below).
2. Describe your idea in a few sentences. The more you say about industry, location, and budget, the sharper the result.
3. Read the verdict and the validation steps, then run the cheap experiments before spending real money.

You don't need to name the skill — Claude recognises a business-idea question.

## Installing the skill

Full step-by-step instructions for every platform (Claude.ai, Claude Desktop, Claude Code) are in the repository's [INSTALL.md](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/blob/main/INSTALL.md).

**The short version (no technical steps):**

1. **[⬇️ Download the skill (ZIP)](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/raw/main/skills/business-idea-validator/business-idea-validator.zip)** — one ready-to-upload file. You don't need to build or zip anything yourself.
2. In Claude, turn on **Code execution and file creation** under **Settings → Capabilities** (one-time).
3. Go to **Customize → Skills**, click **"+" → Create skill → Upload a skill**, and upload the ZIP you just downloaded.
4. Toggle the skill on. Done — start a new chat and describe your idea.

## What it won't do

It gives general information, not financial, legal, or tax advice — for anything binding, see a registered accountant or business adviser, or check business.gov.au. For regulated professions it flags the licensing reality rather than waving it through.
