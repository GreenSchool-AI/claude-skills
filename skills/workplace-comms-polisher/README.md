# Workplace Comms Polisher

A free Claude Skill by AI Courses by Green School that polishes your workplace emails and Slack/Teams messages — shorter, clearer, and pitched right for whoever you're writing to. It can also decode the cryptic message your manager just sent.

## Who it's for

Australian mid-career professionals in banks, consultancies, government, mining services, healthcare admin, and retail head offices — anyone writing 40-80 messages a day who wants to sound sharp without spending 20 minutes on a three-line email.

## What you get

When you paste a draft (Mode A — the default):
1. A one-line read on what's working and what isn't, and how much shorter it can be.
2. Three ready-to-send versions — formal, neutral, and friendly — each with an AU-appropriate greeting and sign-off.
3. Subject line options, for email.
4. Notes on how the message lands in Australian workplace culture.
5. A short "what changed and why" so you learn the pattern, not just get the fix.

When you paste a confusing message from a leader (Mode B):
- What they probably mean, the unspoken subtext, what they actually want from you, and a suggested reply.

It's direct, not sycophantic — Australian workplaces reward that.

## How to use it (non-technical)

1. Open Claude (claude.ai or the desktop app) with this skill added (see "Installing the skill" below).
2. Paste your draft. For a sharper result, say who it's going to (boss / peer / report / client) and which channel.
3. To decode a message instead, paste it and say "translate this from my boss".
4. Pick the version that fits and send.

You don't need to name the skill — Claude recognises a workplace-message request.

## Installing the skill

Full step-by-step instructions for every platform (Claude.ai, Claude Desktop, Claude Code) are in the repository's [INSTALL.md](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/blob/main/INSTALL.md).

**The short version (no technical steps):**

1. **[⬇️ Download the skill (ZIP)](https://github.com/GreenSchool-AI/claude-skills/raw/main/skills/workplace-comms-polisher/workplace-comms-polisher.zip)** — one ready-to-upload file. You don't need to build or zip anything yourself.
2. In Claude, turn on **Code execution and file creation** under **Settings → Capabilities** (one-time).
3. Go to **Customize → Skills**, click **"+" → Create skill → Upload a skill**, and upload the ZIP you just downloaded.
4. Toggle the skill on. Done — start a new chat and paste a draft message.

## What it won't do

It drafts; it doesn't send anything for you. It won't help write something deceptive or build a false paper trail, and for formal HR or legal documents it will point you to the right internal channel.
